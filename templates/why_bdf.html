💡 Why BDF?

BDCOM Devs Forum (BDF) was created to serve as an internal knowledge-sharing hub. It helps team members:

  • 🧠 Share issues they’re facing during development
  • 🔧 Post patches and solutions to common problems
  • 📚 Learn from each other’s work and practices
  • 🗂️ Keep a centralized archive of technical references
  • 💬 Encourage team discussion, feedback, and innovation

With BDF, knowledge no longer remains siloed. It grows, spreads, and stays accessible for everyone.
